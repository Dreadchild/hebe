Usage: %{app} unregister <project[s]>

Description:
  Unregister one or multiple projects from Hebe

Arguments:
  <project[s]>   Unregister one or more projects from Hebe. Unregistering will
                 only stop a project from being part of Hebe, no file will be
                 deleted.
                 You can unregister and register a project as many times as you
                 want.
